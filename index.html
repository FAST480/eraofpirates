About

You have woken up after a severe shipwreck on an island unknown to you, you are trying to remember what happened, but you cannot. There is an unknown man standing next to you who offers to help you remember what happened to you, and you agree without hesitation.
From this moment begins your unforgettable adventure in the world of Era Of Pirates. You have to assemble your team, with which you will learn all the difficulties of pirate life. Fight for valuable resources, glory and honor with other pirates.
Explore all the mysteries and secrets of the mysterious Caribbean Sea and find the legendary Davy Jones chest. Get reputation points and become the most famous pirate, let everyone who hears your name tremble in fear of your greatness. May yer sails stay full and yer powder dry!

Gameplay

Some headline

Assemble your pirate crew and embark on an exciting adventure to surf the seas! Each pirate collects a fixed amount of gold in 15 minutes of your adventure, depending on its rarity. Ships affect the travel time and the number of slots for your team. Create ships of the next level with the help of 5 ships of the previous rarity.

Some headline

There are 2 main resources in the game - gold and rum.Gold is the main resource without which no pirate can live. For gold, you will be able to purchase provisions necessary for expeditions, materials for repairing your ship, packs and much more. A pirate without gold is not a pirate. The port rats who get scared in battle will not get anything, because every pirate must be desperate and brave, but you can't earn money on rum alone. And also gold is the main token of our game. Rum is a resource without which you will not be able to conduct your expedition. The absence of rum on the ship is punishable by a Black Beard, so a sortie without it is impossible.

Some headline

The game world is divided into cells (lands) on which your ship will move. On the cells, you can meet anything from ordinary resources that were brought to the top due to a shipwreck to a mythical beast - the KRAKEN. In the game world, you can meet various NPCs who can help you or deceive you. Therefore, your adventure can become very interesting and exciting, glorify your big name to the whole world!

Some headline

In our game there will be a PVP mode in which you will be able to fight with other players in the present time and earn more tokens than in regular expeditions. In PVP mode, if you lose, your ship will be damaged, and in-game materials such as boards, cloth and iron will be needed to restore it.
